TRADITIONAL ART

So, you have chosen traditional art, wonderful choice! But first off, what is traditional art?

Traditional art is a form of art created using real physical media as opposed to digital art which uses softwares or electronic devices.
Through pencils, markers, brushes, and so much more, artists are able to express their creativive side through the world around them.
Traditional artists are also not simply limited to the 2d world, other traditional artists like wood carvers can create 3d art, which pleases not only the eyes, but the other senses as well.
Goodluck and start your journey onto creating wonderful art!

Painting

Painting is the act of using brushes to apply paint on a solid surface.
Artists may also use other materials other than brushes to spread the paint and
more to create texture and other things the artist may want to use!
Artists also sometimes draw before painting to have a base before they start creating their painting!

Fashion Design

Fashion Design is the art form of creating clothes!
One may create this just forthemselves, cosplays, and selling clothes!
With the artist's skills, they are able to think of aesthetics, designs and certain details
to create their clothes and showcase their art through it!

Sculpture

Scuplture is the art of creating, well, 3d sculptures.
Artists can create models of humans, create pots, and more!
This can be done using wood, marble, clay and more!
Artists can start with just clay and a few materials to carve into the soft clay,
like plastic knives and other objects!

Drawing

Drawing is, well, to create art using a pencil, pen, inks, crayons,
or other type materials other than paints, on a solid surface.
Various instruments can be used to do these and artists
can use pretty much any drawing instrument they may have!
You do not need expensive art supplies to start!
One can just start with crayons, pens or pencils, and a piece of paper!
And even add color to it using crayons or colored pencils you may have lying around!

Paper Craft

Paper Craft is the art of folding paper and creating designs out of it!
An artist may use scissors and glue and more to create their art,
but some do not, like in the popular type of paper craft, Origami.
Artist's can create designs to create designs in paper, like snowflakes, to paper roses to more intricate sculptures!

Woodworking

Woodworking is the art of creating objects out of wood.
This can be sculptures or even furniture!
Artists can create designs and art on the wood and can also carve it to make big or small sculptures!
This is a bit more complicated than the other types above
though as it does take certain tools to carve into wood and also actually have the wood!
